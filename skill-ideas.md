# Skill ideas — future designs

Concept bank for future player skills, from the complementary-skills brainstorm (July 2026).
Each entry is a fantasy + signature mechanic, not a card design.

## The bank

- **Amic de les bèsties** — beast-friend. An animal companion with opinions, plus calming or
  outright turning enemy beasts mid-combat (an alternate way to "win" fights against llops,
  basilisc, manada encounters). Absorbed the earlier Falconeria concept (trained falcon:
  harass, spot, dive on command).
- **Hipnosi** — mesmerist mind control (Guild Wars Mesmer + Etrian Odyssey Hexer). Meet an
  enemy's gaze and for one moment its axe is yours: it strikes its own neighbour, or freezes
  mid-stride. The "enemy as your weapon" verb.
- **Canviaformes** — shapeshifter. While transformed, your cards ARE the beast's cards; your
  equipment, tools and trinkets don't work with paws. Total commitment, round by round.
- **Miratges** — illusionist decoys: phantom soldiers that walk, breathe and draw steel.
  Enemies must treat them as real; every axe that splits a mirage missed a person. Deception
  as a defensive resource measured in wasted enemy turns.
- **Punts de pressió** — dim-mak pressure-point monk (Neji from Naruto; Etrian Odyssey's bind
  system). Negligible damage: two fingers to the shoulder and the sword-arm hangs dead (no
  attacks); the knee (no dodging); the throat (no focus). Switch enemies off one limb at a time.
- **Furt** — mid-combat thief. Not dagger damage: fingers in the enemy's belt while it swings.
  Steal the shaman's fetish (curses die), the captain's horn (no reinforcements), the thing a
  boss's power hangs from. The verb nobody else has: taking things away.
- **Invocador** — true summoner, deliberately narrow: ONE contracted entity called by its
  pact-name, standing on the field. Dismissable and recallable; while it stands, part of your
  strength lives outside your body — killing it hurts you.
- **Verins** — the poisoner, dark mirror of the Metge: same chemistry, opposite oath. Slow
  venoms that bloom rounds later, paralytics, coated blades — and antidote-knowledge as a
  side effect.

## Taste guardrails (learned the hard way)

Concepts that work are **classic, instantly-visual archetypes** with a distinctive mechanical
verb and a serious tone. Rejected repeatedly: shout/number buffs, banners, warleader chants,
teacher/coach, taunts, blood pacts, luck/dice-meta, tempo/drums, fire-spread, puppeteer
threads, oaths, ward zones, mirrors, masks, forge, rust, chains, wind-knots, true names, nets,
clay figurines, dowser, cook, flask-bearer, ancestor spirits (too close to the Nigromant),
echo knight, sentinel/overwatch, blue-mage learning, partner bonds, exorcist/dispel,
enlarge/reduce, always-first lightning.

Design rule as always: lore first, mechanics second (see CLAUDE.md) — when one of these gets
picked up, design the actions the fantasy demands and only then figure out effect handlers.
